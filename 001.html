13th Hour Vol. 1

Subjoi & Ricky Razu

2018


Support: 13th Hour Shop / Stream links

Our debut EP was almost a year in the making: a four track compilation celebrating both the pulse of the late-night dancefloor and the starry-eyed ride home. Two tracks from Subjoi forge the A-side, the vibrant yet moody “Make Me” taking the A1 slot and spacy, hi-hat bonanza “Doin’ Fine” at A2. And influenced by the bass-heavy vibrato of 70s funk, Ricky Razu kicks off the B-side with the infinitely groovable “Reaching Out,” followed by the sultry cut “Moonlight Jam” to close out the album.
