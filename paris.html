Paris

deRijk

2022


Support: 13th Hour shop / Vinyl & Stream links

At its best, music is a feeling. Even before the first note hits the experience is already alive; dropping the needle onto a new record for the first time, the room’s dust particles serenading you through the silence of the run-in groove. And then, all in an instant, immeasurable change.

At its best, music isn’t really worth talking about–it seems somewhat futile not to just be listening, drinking it in. That feeling. As music lovers, we are all ultimately chasing that feeling. We don’t want to think about anything, we want the music to sweep over us, confuse us, energize us, and leave us with a glass that is both half full and half empty. This track, this side, this album will just have to remain on repeat, because what else could possibly fill the silent void of the run-out groove?

DJ Counselling is no stranger to this feeling. His music seeks to strip everything else away so there is only one thing left: the song. Sounds from space; a transmission from a different galaxy; a dystopic vignette quickly turned to euphoria; an unsettling and yet strangely uplifting melody. These are just some of the themes explored across DJ Counselling’s Caring EP, a record that, across four tracks, covers vast expanses of the musical landscape. Trying for any description more precise than that is fundamentally pointless–it’s a journey as much as it’s a collection of songs.
