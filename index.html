13th Hour Records is a New York based record label. Initially started by two friends looking for dance music on the darker end of the spectrum, it has since evolved into an eclectic outlet for experimental, ambient and electronic sounds.

We are humbled every day by the artists who choose to work with us and by the listeners who choose to join us on this journey. We feel like we're just getting started.

You’re welcome to get in touch with us at info@13thhourrecs.com. We devote most of our time to serving the artists currently on our roster, but we’re always open to listening to new music.
